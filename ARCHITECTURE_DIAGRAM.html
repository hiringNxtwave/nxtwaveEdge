01
The Problem

What's broken today?

The current hiring process relies on manual tools that slow down both the NxtWave team and hiring companies.

Current Pain

Excel Sheets Shared via WhatsApp

Candidate data exported to spreadsheets and shared over informal channels. No search, no filtering, poor client impression.

Current Pain

Feedback Lost in Phone Calls

Recruiter feedback happens over calls and WhatsApp — unstructured, untracked, and impossible to reference later.

Current Pain

Interview Scheduling Chaos

15+ interview rounds with constant back-and-forth to check availability. No centralized calendar or tracking.

Current Pain

No Visibility for Either Side

Admin doesn't know recruiter progress. Recruiter doesn't know if new candidates are coming. No shared dashboard.

06
Access & Permissions

Who Can Do What

Akanksha manages the full loop — from CRM data to candidate placement. Recruiters focus on their job boards.

Akanksha — Admin

  • ✓ Import candidate sheets to any company job
  • ✓ Review curated pool before sending
  • ✓ Send candidates to recruiter (creates account)
  • ✓ Update pipeline status and add notes
  • ✓ View all job boards across all companies
  • ✓ Add new candidate batches to existing jobs
  • ✓ Manage HubSpot CRM — contacts, companies, deals
  • ✓ Sync deal stages with hiring pipeline

Recruiter — Company Account

  • ✓ View curated candidates for their jobs only
  • ✓ Move candidates between pipeline stages
  • ✓ Schedule interviews from candidate card
  • ✓ Add notes and feedback per candidate
  • ✓ Compare candidates side-by-side
  • ✓ Download screening reports (PDF)
